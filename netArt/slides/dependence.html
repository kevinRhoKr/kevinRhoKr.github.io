Our Dependence on Google

"You now know, consciously or unconsciously, that Google is not God. That Google is not the absolute, untouchable, clear thing it pretends to be."

-Domenico Quaranta


Is it true that we are thinking of Google as God?

Google is the most visited website (89.3 billion visits per month in 2022)
Google has 91.9% of the search engine market as of January 2022
Almost half of product searches, 46%, begin on Google
Google processes over 8.5 billion searches per day as of January 2022
